Kitten Cuisine

From the Alley to the Oven!

Here at Kitten Cuisine, we like cats...

We Like them Fresh!

Every night, our team of highly trained cat catchers roam the streets and return with gunnysacks bulging with stray cats.

We Like them Fat!

These lucky kitties then get to spend a few months in our Kozy Kat Hotel until they're nice and plump and ready for the kitchen!
We like them Grilled!
With our super secret blend of "herbs" and "spices", that stray kitty becomes a savory dish that isn't quite fit for a king, but is more than acceptable for a duke or an earl!
We like them Green!

Best of all, by eating here, you are directly contributing to our efforts to reduce the stray cat population that is harming our environment.Feel free to brag about it on social media!
